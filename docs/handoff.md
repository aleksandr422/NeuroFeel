# MindDiary Handoff

## Implemented across prompts 1-5

- Unified mood scale and deterministic summary generation.
- Design tokens and reusable UI primitives.
- IA split: marketing vs authenticated app routes.
- Global write action and onboarding flow.
- Safety layer:
  - local crisis signal detector (RU/EN)
  - support resources card with accessible polite announcement
  - scope disclaimers surfaced in high-risk UX points
  - banned marketing phrase audit in CI
  - review-date freshness checks for lexicon/resources

## Intentionally still out of scope

- Server sync and multi-device account recovery.
- Real LLM mental-health analysis or professional triage.
- Clinical content review pipeline and legal-compliance workflow.
- Full threat protection against compromised devices/malware.
